Training Highlights

  • 100 community members trained
  • Basic life support skills
  • Emergency response procedures
  • Practical hands-on sessions
  • Certification provided

Program Content

The comprehensive first aid training program covered essential life-saving techniques, including CPR, wound care, and emergency response protocols. Participants engaged in hands-on practice sessions with professional instructors.

"Empowering community members with first aid skills creates a safer environment for everyone." - Program Director

Community Impact

With 100 newly trained first responders, our community is better prepared to handle medical emergencies. The program has significantly improved emergency response capabilities at the local level.

Future Programs

Building on this success, we plan to expand the program to reach more communities and include advanced training modules for previous participants.
